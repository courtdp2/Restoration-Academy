Academic Philosophy


The goal of the school is to meet the academic, spiritual, and social needs of each student at our school in a Christ-centered environment. We believe that the school has been called to a unique mission to minister to and meet the needs of a predominantly urban and at-risk student body.

Rational

to develop and utilize cirtical thinking skills (Gen. 1:1-23, 26)

Physical

to respect and honor one another and hold human life in high regard (I Cor. 6:19-20)

Relational

to foster an environment of shalom with true peace and harmony in all our relationships (Gal. 4:4-7)

Creative

to create and appreciate beauty (Gen. 1:10b)

Faithful

to introduce each student to Christ and for each student to name Him as Savior and Lord (Ex. 20:3)(Rom. 1:22-23, 25)

Disciplined

to foster discipline that rescues students from death, and introduces them to the robust love of Christ (Heb. 12:6)(Prov. 14:12)

Diligent

to be responsible, hard working, and to earn their grades and reputation through sweat equity (Prov. 12:24)

Emotional

to love one another as Christ has loved us (Jn. 15:12)

Bright Futures

College and Career Program

Our College and Career program is three fold: mentoring for access to college, mentoring for persistence in college and mentoring for success in life. Through partnerships with a variety of entities across the Birmingham area and the state of Alabama, Restoration Academy is able to introduce students to a wide variety of experiences and experts in their field.

For the past 11 years, 100% of our graduates have been accepted into college, but it doesn’t end there. Our goal isn’t to send a student off into the world after high school graduation with a diploma in one hand and an acceptance letter in the other… we walk with them well through their graduation from college or whatever path the Lord has for them.
