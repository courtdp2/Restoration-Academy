Loving God with...


…all our minds

We want your student to learn to "love God with all of their minds." We have rigorous curriculum, dedicated and called instructors, and the facilities to help your students grow in their education. For the last 10 years in a row, all of our seniors have been admitted to college.


…all our strength

We want for your student to learn to "love God with their strength as well." We have excellent physical education classes. And we offer a variety of sports including basketball, baseball, girls tennis and volleyball, peewee soccer, and even Brazilian jiu-jitsu.


…all our hearts

We desire for your student to learn to "love God with all of their heart." We offer a strong fine arts program that includes elementary drama, visual arts classes, and an award-winning high school concert chorale that travels around the state.
